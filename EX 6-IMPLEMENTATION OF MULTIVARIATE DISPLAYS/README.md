## Overview
This experiment demonstrates the use of **multivariate visualization techniques** to analyze complex retail data. Using the **R programming language**, multiple variables such as **Sales, Profit, Discount, and Region** are visualized simultaneously to identify patterns, relationships, and insights useful for business analytics and AI-driven decision-making.

---

## Objective
To implement advanced **multivariate displays** for analyzing complex datasets and understanding relationships among multiple variables.

---

## Problem Scenario
A **retail analytics firm** analyzes sales performance across regions to:
- Study relationships between sales, profit, and discounts  
- Compare performance across regions  
- Support business optimization strategies
